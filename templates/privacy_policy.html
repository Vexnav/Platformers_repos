Privacy Policy

The DUT Lost and Found Portal respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal information. This policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your data.

Information We Collect

  • Personal details like your name and contact information when you report lost or found items.
  • Item details and images to facilitate item recovery.
  • Feedback, reviews, and other data voluntarily provided by users.

How We Use Your Information

  • To help match lost and found items with their rightful owners.
  • To improve the functionality and user experience of the portal.
  • To maintain records and ensure platform security.

Data Security

We take all reasonable measures to protect your data from unauthorized access, alteration, and destruction. However, no system is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute protection.

Contact Us

If you have any questions about our privacy practices, please contact us at support@dutlostandfound.com.
