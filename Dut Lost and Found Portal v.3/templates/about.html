About Us

The Durban University of Technology (DUT) Lost and Found Portal is a cutting-edge platform designed to reunite lost belongings with their rightful owners. Whether you're a student, staff member, or visitor, our portal serves as a reliable resource for recovering lost items in a timely and efficient manner.

Why Choose the DUT Lost and Found Portal?

Losing personal belongings can be stressful, but the DUT Lost and Found Portal eliminates the hassle of finding lost items. By offering a centralized, technology-driven solution, we ensure that misplaced items are easy to report and claim.

  • User-Friendly Interface: Our platform is simple to use, even for first-time users.
  • Fast Reporting: Quickly list an item you've found or report an item you've lost.
  • Real-Time Matching: Advanced algorithms match lost and found items based on detailed descriptions and location data.
  • Secure Portal: All your information is handled with care and security.

How It Works

  1. Report: List an item you’ve lost or found by entering detailed information.
  2. Match: The system automatically finds possible matches based on the provided details.
  3. Connect: Contact the person who found your item (or the rightful owner) to arrange for its return.

Contact Us

If you have any questions or need assistance, please contact our Lost and Found Help Desk located on campus or email us at lostandfound@dut.ac.za.
