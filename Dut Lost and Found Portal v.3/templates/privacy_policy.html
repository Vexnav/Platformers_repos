Privacy Policy

At the Durban University of Technology (DUT) Lost and Found Portal (the "Portal"), we value your privacy and are committed to safeguarding your personal information. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, share, and protect your data.

Information We Collect

  • Personal Information: Includes your name, email address, contact number, and any other details you provide during account creation or item reporting.
  • Item Details: Information related to lost or found items, such as descriptions, images, locations, and the dates they were reported.
  • Platform Interactions: Data regarding how you use the Portal, including search queries, feedback submissions, and user-generated content.
  • Device and Connection Data: Your IP address, browser type, and operating system for improving security and troubleshooting technical issues.

How We Use Your Information

We use the information collected from you to provide, enhance, and maintain the services of the Portal. This includes:

  • Item Matching: Connecting users who have lost items with those who have found them based on submitted details.
  • Service Improvement: Using feedback and interaction data to refine and optimize the Portal's functionality.
  • Communication: Sending updates, notifications, and alerts regarding your account or reported items.
  • Security: Monitoring and analyzing activities to ensure compliance with security policies and prevent unauthorized usage.

How We Share Your Information

We only share your personal information under the following circumstances:

  • With Other Users: When necessary to facilitate communication between item owners and finders (e.g., sharing contact details with your consent).
  • Compliance: To comply with legal requirements, such as law enforcement requests or university regulations.
  • Service Providers: With trusted third parties who assist us in operating the Portal (e.g., hosting or email services). These providers are bound by confidentiality agreements.

Data Retention

We retain your personal information for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined in this policy or to comply with legal obligations. Once this period has expired, we securely delete or anonymize your data.

Your Rights

As a user of the DUT Lost and Found Portal, you have the following rights regarding your personal information:

  • Access: Request access to the personal information we hold about you.
  • Correction: Update or correct your personal data to ensure accuracy.
  • Deletion: Request that your data be deleted from our records, subject to legal and contractual limitations.
  • Consent Withdrawal: Withdraw consent for the use of your data in specific circumstances.

Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your data from unauthorized access, alteration, and loss. However, no digital platform is completely secure, and users should take precautions when sharing sensitive information.

Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy is subject to periodic updates. Any significant changes will be communicated through email or notifications on the Portal. Continued use of the Portal after updates indicates acceptance of the revised policy.

Contact Us

For questions, concerns, or requests regarding your personal information, please contact our support team: support@dutlostandfound.com.
