About Us

The Durban University of Technology Lost and Found Portal is a community-focused initiative aimed at connecting people with their lost belongings. By leveraging modern technology, the platform ensures efficiency, transparency, and reliability in helping students and staff recover their items.

Our Mission

To provide a user-friendly platform that simplifies the process of finding lost items and reporting found items, fostering a sense of community and mutual responsibility.

Our Vision

A supportive and engaged university environment where misplaced items are quickly and easily returned to their rightful owners.

Core Values

  • Integrity and trust.
  • Innovation and reliability.
  • Community and collaboration.
